Privacy Policy

Schelling Points — last updated March 6, 2026

What we collect

We collect the minimum data needed to run the game:

  • Nickname — the display name you choose when joining a game. This is not tied to any account or real identity.
  • Push notification token — if you enable notifications, we store your device token so we can notify you when it's your turn. You can revoke this at any time in your device settings.
  • Game responses — the answers you submit during gameplay. These are held in memory during an active game and are not persisted after the game ends.

What we don't collect

We do not collect email addresses, real names, locations, analytics, advertising identifiers, or any other personal information. There are no accounts. We do not use cookies or third-party trackers.

Data storage

Push notification tokens are stored in a local SQLite database on our server. Game state is held in memory and discarded when a game ends. We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page with the new date.
